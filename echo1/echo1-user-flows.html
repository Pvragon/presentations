Finance Specialist owns the full billing lifecycle from transport review through claims submission and remittance. They have read-only access to transport records for billing context, and manage all payer/billing configuration.
Note: Dispatchers do not have billing access — amounts, invoices, claims, and reimbursement rates are Finance-only.

Finance Specialist Journeys

Finance Specialist also has read access to Ops Reporting (UC-08a, UC-08d) and client insurance data for claims processing.

Client Administrators are leadership/designated staff at partner organizations (requesting entities). They see their own organization's transport activity only — no other tenant data, no PHI beyond what's operationally necessary, no financial data, no employee data. Personas doc explicitly states: "minimal feature set at launch — full self-service capabilities are post-launch."
